Four Ways In

Kerala doesn't fit on one postcard.

Pick the version that matches your pace — they're often only an hour or two apart from each other.

Backwaters

Vembanad, Punnamada and the slow canals of Alappuzha. Houseboats, paddy fields below sea level, herons that don't startle.

Alappuzha · Kottayam · Kollam

Highlands

Munnar's tea hills, Wayanad's spice slopes, Vagamon's wind. Cool air above 1,500m, where the Western Ghats earn their UNESCO listing.

Idukki · Wayanad · Kottayam

Heritage

Fort Kochi's tiled warehouses, Mattancherry's spice godowns, murals older than most nations. A trading post since long before the phrase existed.

Ernakulam · Thrissur

Coast

Kovalam's crescent, Varkala's cliffs, Marari's quiet fishing villages. 600km of Arabian Sea, and almost none of it crowded.

Thiruvananthapuram · Kozhikode
